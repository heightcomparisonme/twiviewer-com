## Understanding Advertising Metrics

### What is CPM?

CPM (Cost Per Mille) is the cost an advertiser pays for one thousand advertisement impressions. It's one of the most important metrics in digital advertising.

**CPM Formula:**
```
CPM = (Total Cost / Total Impressions) × 1,000
```
